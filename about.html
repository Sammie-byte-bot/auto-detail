The Beginning

Our Founding Story

At AutoDetail Car Wash, we're passionate about providing exceptional car care experiences. Founded in 2010 by car enthusiast and entrepreneur Alex Chen, our company has grown to become a trusted name in the industry — known for meticulous attention to detail and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

With a team of certified technicians and a dedication to using only the highest-quality products and equipment, we strive to exceed our clients' expectations with every single visit. What started as a one-bay operation has grown to a full-service facility processing thousands of vehicles annually.
